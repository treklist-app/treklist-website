How to Set Up the Treklist Share Extension

  1. Open Instagram and find a post you want to save.
  2. Tap the Share icon (paper plane or three dots).
  3. Select Share to... from the options.
  4. Swipe through the app icons to the end and tap More.
  5. Find Treklist in the list and tap the "+" or "Add to Favorites" to make it a suggested app.
  6. Next time, just tap Treklist to save the post!
